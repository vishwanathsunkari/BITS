Memory Game MIND YOUR OWN GAME A classic memory game with pairs of matching cards.
  • Rules:
  • This Game is test your memory power , what you can observe within 5 seconds of time
  • so, you can able to see the total number for only 5 seconds
  • once the timer goes off, you can able to enter the number in the input box and the actual number is not visible
  • the number must be entered and you can't enter more than 9 numbers
  • There are Three buttons "Verify", "Clear" and "Exit"
  • Verify will be enabled once , you enter a valid number
  • If you want to clear the number entered, you can click on Clear button
  • On clicking Exit button , it will take you to Home page.
  • Once you Enter the correct number , you can see your score increasing starting from 0
  • If you enter wrong number , you will lose one point from your score , so be careful and MIND YOUR OWN MIND
